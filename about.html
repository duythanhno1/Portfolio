“Electric” is a company specializing in providing electronic devices to users and bringing the highest and most optimal use value to users.


Electronic devices are devices, items with basic structure including semiconductor components and electronic circuits. These electronic devices will most likely not have mechanical operations. Electronic equipment works on automation. And apply modern technologies to operate. Electronic devices make people's lives much more convenient and easier. Electronic devices appear everywhere in life. Electronic devices are indispensable items in today's modern society.


Due to consumer demand for electronic devices is increasing and constantly innovating. Therefore, we established the company to serve the needs of customers in increasing productivity and efficiency, helping to speed up the production process.


The company was established on June 20, 2023, specializing in electronic devices such as graphics cards, cpu, computer cases, monitors, components, ... In addition, the establishment of a company also means to help improve people's quality of life because thanks to electronic devices, society is more and more developed and modern.
